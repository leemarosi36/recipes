PB&J Sandwich

by Layla Cecil

Ingredients

  • 2 slices sandwich bread
  • 2 tablespoons peanut butter
  • 2 teaspoons jelly

Directions

  1. Spread peanut butter on one slice of bread
  2. Spread jelly on the other slice of bread
  3. Put the two pieces of bread together to make a sandwich
