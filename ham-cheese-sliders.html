Recipe Name

by Brendan Warren

Ingredients

  • 12-count package Hawaiian sweet rolls
  • 3/4 pound cooked deli ham
  • 3/4 pound Swiss cheese
  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter, melted
  • 1 tablespoon dijon mustard & poppy seeds
  • 2 teaspoons dried minced onions & Worcesteshire sauce
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt & pepper

Directions

  1. Preheat oven to 350F. Line a 9x9-inch or 9x13-inch pan with aluminum foil, spray with cooking spray
  2. Using a knife, slice the rolls in half so you have a "slab" of tops and a "slab" of bottoms. DON'T pull the rolls apart and slice individually because you want to keep them connected
  3. Place the bottom "slab" of rolls in the prepared pan. Evenly layer about half of the ham over the rolls. Evenly layer the cheese. Evenly layer the remaining ham. Now add the top "slab" of rolls.
  4. To a medium microwave-safe bowl, add the butter and heat on high power to melt, about 1 minute. Add the mustard, poppy seeds, onions, Worcesteshire sauce, salt, pepper, and whisk to combine.
  5. Slowly pour the mixture over the rolls. Cover with aluminum foil and allow rolls to stand at room temp for about 5 to 10 minutes.
  6. Bake covered for about 20 minutes or until cheese has melted. Then, uncover and cover for about 3-5 minutes or until as done as desired.
