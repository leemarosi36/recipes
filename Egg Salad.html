Egg Salad Sandwich

by Leemarosi Tamilarasan

Ingredients

  • 8 eggs
  • 1/2 cup mayonnaise
  • 1/4 chopped green onion
  • 1 teaspoon yellow mustard
  • Salt and Pepper to tate
  • 1/4 teaspoon paprika

Directions

  1. Step:1

    2. Place egg in a saucepan and cover with cold water. Bring water to a boil and immediately remove from heat. Cover and let eggs stand in hot water for 10 to 12 minutes. Remove from hot water, cool, peel and chop.

  2. Step:2

    3. Place the chopped eggs in a bowl, and stir in the mayonnaise, mustard and green onion. Season with salt, pepper and paprika. Stir and serve on your favorite bread or crackers.
